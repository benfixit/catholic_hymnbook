8. Draw us nearer, Lord, each to each,

Draw us nearer, Lord, each to each,
O Lord, each to each.

9. Fill our hearts, Lord, with your care,
Fill our hearts, Lord, with your care,
Fill our hearts, Lord, with your care,
O Lord, with your care.

10. Bless us, Lord, with your Love,
