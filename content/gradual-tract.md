---
id: 118
title: Gradual/Tract (Misa pro defunctis - Mass for the dead)

---

1. V. Requiem aeternam dona eis, Domine,
et lux perpetua luceat eis.

R. In memoria aeterna erit justus, ab auditione
mala non timebit
Absolve, Domine, animas amnium fidelium
defunctorum, ab omni vincula delictorum

Et gratia tua illis succurente: mereatur
evadere iudicium ultionis

Et lucis aeterna beautitudine perfrui.
