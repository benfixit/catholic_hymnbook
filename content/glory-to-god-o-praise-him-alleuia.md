title: Glory to God O Praise Him, Alleuia
---

_Refrain:_
_Glory to God, Glory, O praise Him, alleluia!_
_Glory to God, Glory, O praise the name of the Lord._

1. Bless the Lord all you works of the Lord
Praise and glorify Him forever.

2. Sun and moon bless the Lord
Bless the Lord you stars of heaven.

3. Light and darkness bless the Lord
Bless the Lord you nights and days.

4. Now let the earth bless the Lord
Praise and glorify Him forever.

5. Mountains and hills bless the Lord
Bless the Lord you seas and rivers.

6. Now let the Church bless the Lord
Let His people praise His name.

7. Bless the Lord you saints of the Lord
Peoples near and far, praise His name.

8. Praise to the Father and to the Son,
To the Holy Spirit Three in One.
