title: You Are the Honour
---

_Chorus:_
_You are the honour_
_You are the glory of our people_
_Holy Virgin Mary._

1. You are the glory of Jerusalem
Holy Virgin Mary.

2. You are the greatest joy of Israel
Holy Virgin Mary.

3. You are the highest honour of our race
Holy Virgin Mary.

4. May you be blessed by the Lord Most High
Holy Virgin Mary.

5. Now, and for all ages without end
Holy Virgin Mary.

6. Give praise to God in the Church, and Christ
Holy Virgin Mary.
