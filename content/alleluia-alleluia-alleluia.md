title: Alleluia! Alleluia! Alleluia!
category: entrance
---

1. Alleluia, Alleluia, Alleluia,
Praise the Lord for it's good.
Believe in God, Alleluia,
Praise the Lord for it's good.
For He is the Lord, Lord God of all; Alleluia;
Praise the Lord for it's good.

2. Adore the Lord, Alleluia;
Alleluia; Praise the Lord for it's good.
All you His creatures, Alleluia.
Alleluia; Praise the Lord for it's good.
Ask His forgiveness, alleluia.
Alleluia; Praise the Lord for it's good.
For His mercy is without end.
Alleluia; Praise the Lord for it's good.

3. Give thanks to God, alleluia.
Alleluia; Praise the Lord for it's good.
For all His favours to all mankind
Alleluia; Praise the Lord for it's good.
Ask for your need alleluia
Alleluia; Praise the Lord for it's good.

4. Ask you shall receive;
Knock, He shall open;
Seek, you shall find;
Praise the Lord, for it's good.
Glory be to the Father and to the Son,
and to the Spirit, now for ever more, Alleluia.
