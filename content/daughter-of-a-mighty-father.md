title: Daughter of a Mighty Father

---

_Chorus:_
_Mácula non est in te,_
_Mácula non est in te._
_Mácula non est in te,_
_Mácula non est in te._

1. Daughter of a mighty Father,
Stainless Maiden Queen of May;
Angel forms around thee gather;
Mácula non est in te.

2. Mother of the Son and Savior,
Of the Truth, the Life, the Way;
Guide our footsteps, calm our passions:
Mácula non est in te.

3. Spouse of the Eternal Spirit,
Listen to our earnest lay;
Grant we may thy love inherit:
Mácula non est in te.

4. Brightest Star in heaven's glory,
Let thy splendor light our way;
Guide and help us, love and bless us:
Mácula non est in te.
