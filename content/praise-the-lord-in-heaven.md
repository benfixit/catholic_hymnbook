---
id: 320
title: Praise the Lord in Heaven
category: recessional
---

1. Praise the Lord in Heaven,
Praise Him in His holy place,
Praise Him for His greatness,
Praise Him for His powerful deeds.

2. Praise the Lord with trumpets
Praise Him with a thousand strings,
Praise the Lord with dancing,
Praise Him with joyful shout.

3. Praise the Lord with cymbals,
Praise Him with resounding brass,
Praise Him, all the living,
Praise the Lord for evermore.

4. Praise the glorious Father ,
Praise the everlasting Son,
Praise the Holy Spirit,
Praise and glory, Three in One.
