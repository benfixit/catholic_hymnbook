---
id: 157
title: Holy, Holy is the Lord (Psalm 99)
---

_Chorus:_
_Holy, holy is the Lord_
_Holy, mighty, holy is the Lord._

1. Tremble, for the Lord is King
He's ruling over every thing,
So bring Him glory, let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.

2. Sisters, brothers, let us shout His praise
Praising His holy name all the day,
So bring Him glory let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.

3. Justice is what our God loves
Justice that always comes from above,
So bring Him glory, let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.

4. Thank Him for His guiding hand,
Ask Him to bless our Father's land,
And bring Him glory, let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.

5. Aaron called His mighty name,
He came all in a pillar of flame,
So bring Him glory, let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.

6. Moses felt the Lord close by,
Giving a help from on high,
So bring Him glory, let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.

7. Millions called His mighty name,
He answered ev'ry-one, just the same,
So bring Him glory, let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.

8. Hail Him who is King on high,
Love Him who hears our every cry,
And bring Him glory, let us sing His glory;
Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King:
Hosanna! Blessings let us bring unto a very mighty King.
