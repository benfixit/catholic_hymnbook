title: The Angel Gabriel
category: entrance

_Chorus:_
_"Most highly favored lady!" Gloria!_

1. The angel Gabriel from heaven came,
His wings as drifted snow, his eyes as flame;
"All hail", said he, "O lowly maiden Mary",

2. "For know a blessed Mother you shall be,
All generations praise continually,
Your Son shall be Emmanuel, by seers fortold."

3. Then gentle Mary meekly bowed her head;
"To me be as it pleases God!" she said.
"My soul shall laud and magnify his holy name."

4. Of her, Emmanuel, the Christ, was born
In Bethlehem, all on a Christmas morn;
And Christian folk throughout the world will ever say:
