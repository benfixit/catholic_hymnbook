title: Magnify, Glorify, Extol and Exalt the Lord
category: entrance
---

1. Magnify, glorify, extol and exalt the Lord;
Magnify, glorify His holy name for evermore;
Magnify, glorify, extol and exalt the Lord;
Exalt and glorify,
Magnify His name for ever more,
Our feet shall stand within thy gates, O Jerusalem!

2. Rejoice in thy salvation,
Our feet shall stand within thy gates, O Jerusalem
Rejoice in thy salvation,
Our feet shall stand within thy gates
O Jerusalem! shall stand within thy gates,
O Jerusalem! Magnify, glorify, extol and exalt the Lord.

3. Magnify, glorify His holy name forever more;
Magnify, glorify, extol and exalt the Lord;
Exalt and glorify,
Magnify His name for ever more
Extol Him that rideth upon the heav'ns,
By His name, JAH!

4. Extol Him that rideth upon the heav'ns,
By His name, JAH!
And rejoice and rejoice and rejoice before Him.
We will rejoice in Your salvation,
And in Your name will we lift up our banners
And come again with singing unto Zion.

5. And obtain gladness and joy,
We will rejoice in Your salvation
And in Your name will we set up our banners,
And come again with singing unto Zion,
And obtain gladness and joy Halleluia!
Amen, Alleluia! Amen! Alleluia! Alleluia! Amen.
