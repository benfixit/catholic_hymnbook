---
id: 45
title: Blessed Are the Poor in Spirit

---

1. Blessed are the poor in spirit;
Blessed are the poor in spirit;
Blessed are the poor in spirit;
Theirs is the kingdom of God.

_Chorus:_
_On that mountain, Jesus talking with us;_
_On that mountain, Jesus walking with us;_
_On that mountain, halleluia!_
_Halle, halleluia_
_Sing out all you children of the Lord._

2. Blessed is the man who is humble;
Blessed is the man who is humble;
Blessed is the man who is humble;
He'll win possession of the land.

3. Blessed are the ones now in mourning;
Blessed are the ones now in mourning;
Blessed are the ones now in mourning;
They'll have their comfort one day.

4. Blessed all who thirst for justice;
Blessed all who thirst for justice;
Blessed all who thirst for justice;
For they will soon be satisfied.

5. Blessed is the man of mercy;
Blessed is the man of mercy;
Blessed is the man of mercy;
he will have mercy in his turn.

6. Blessed are the clean of heart;
Blessed are the clean of heart;
Blessed are the clean of heart;
They will see the face of the Lord.

7. Blessed are the peacemakers;
Blessed are the peacemakers;
Blessed are the peacemakers;
they are the children of their God.

8. Blessed are the sons of justice;
Blessed are the sons of justice;
Blessed are the sons of justice;
theirs is the kingdom of the Lord.
