title: The Joy of the Lord
---

_Chorus:_
_The joy of the Lord, our rampart and power!_

1. Blessed are You, O merciful Lord
Praised by all men for ages to come.

2. O Lord on high, Your glorious name,
Let all the earth proclaim without end.

3. Glory and power to Father and Son,
And to the Spirit, joy of our life.
