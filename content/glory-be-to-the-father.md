---
id: 178
title: Glory Be to the Father
category: entrance
---

1. Glory be to the Father,
Glory be to the Son,
And to the Holy Spirit,
Now and forever, Amen.

2. His mercy lives forever,
Glory be to God,
His wisdom dulls the wisdom of men,
Now and forever, Amen.

3. Oh, sing His praises o'er the land,
Glory be to God,
And with your voices sound His name,
Now and forever, Amen.

4. His love endures forever,
Glory be to God,
His goodness knows no limit,
Now and forever, Amen.
